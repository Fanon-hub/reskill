FAQs & Help

General Questions

  • What is ReSKILL?
    ReSKILL is an online learning platform offering courses in various fields of study.
  • How do I sign up for an account?
    Click on the 'Sign Up' button and follow the instructions to create a new account.
  • How do I reset my password?
    Use the 'Forgot Password' option on the login page to receive a reset link.

Courses and Learning

  • How do I enroll in a course?
    Go to the course page and click 'Enroll Now'.
  • Can I preview a course before enrolling?
    Yes, you can preview the syllabus and some sample lessons.
  • What happens if I don’t complete a course?
    You can continue the course at any time, but certificates are only awarded once all requirements are met.

Payments and Subscriptions

  • What payment methods are accepted?
    We accept credit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment methods.
  • Can I get a refund if I’m not satisfied?
    Yes, refunds are available under our refund policy within a set timeframe.
  • How does the subscription plan work?
    Subscriptions are charged monthly/annually, and you can cancel at any time.

Technical Issues

  • The platform isn’t loading; what should I do?
    Try refreshing the page or check your internet connection. If the issue persists, contact support.
  • How do I update my profile or change my email address?
    Go to your profile settings and make the necessary changes.

Contact Support

If you need further assistance, please contact us.
